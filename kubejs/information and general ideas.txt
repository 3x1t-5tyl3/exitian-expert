Rarity types:

Storage blocks and nuggets inherit rarity.

1 - lowest, stuff like lead, iron ect
2 - low tier base alloys - stuff like brass, bronze ect
3 - rare metals and their alloys - platinum, gold, silver ect
4 - rare alloys - enderium (not electrum) ect.
5 - magical ingots - neptunium, manasteel ect
6 - High level ingots like tyrian
7 - end-game ingots like the ultiamte ingot

Harvest level:

1 - Wood
2 - gold
3 - stone
4 - Iron
5 - Diamond
6 - netherite
7 - amethyst
